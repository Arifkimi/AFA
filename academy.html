AFA ACADEMY




 AFA academy was established on 25 January 2022 with the purpose of teaching and training new 
    students with the techniques of cutting Men's.
    This initiave was founded by the the owners of AFA Barber & Saloon , 
    Mr. Mohd Firdaus, Mr. Mohd Aliff Nadzmi and Mr. Wan Arif Hakimi
    to assist those who are eager to change their life
    by shaping their future direction with the career of barber.





The course training program offered by AFA Academy will only take 2 months. 
  The students will be trained by highly skilled instructors which is our owners itself!

The course will be conducted on Monday to Friday 
from 10 am to 10 pm
and holidays are every Saturday and Sunday.

The intern will also be given a key benchmark (KPI) which is to serve 100 customers within 1 month.
